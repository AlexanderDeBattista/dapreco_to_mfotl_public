Article:
Certification bodies
Paragraph:
The certification bodies referred to in paragraph 1 shall provide the competent supervisory authorities with the reasons for granting or withdrawing the requested certification.

Statement 0
Raw Dapreco, Compact
EXISTS a1 . ep . edp . w . z . x . acm . eac . (rioOnto:RexistAtTime(a1, t1) AND 'rioOnto:and(a1, ep, ead, edp) AND prOnto:DataSubject(w) AND prOnto:PersonalData(z, w) AND prOnto:Controller(y, z) AND prOnto:Processor(x) AND 'prOnto:nominates(edp, y, x) AND 'prOnto:PersonalDataProcessing(ep, x, z) AND 'dapreco:AdhereTo(ead, y, acm) AND dapreco:ApprovedCertificationMechanism(acm) AND dapreco:BodyACM(b) AND 'dapreco:AccreditedBy(eac, b, fun(prOnto:SupervisoryAuthority(fun(prOnto:MemberState(y)))))) IMPLIES EXISTS en . t2 . l . (rioOnto:RexistAtTime(en, t2) AND after(t2, t1) AND 'prOnto:Communicate(en, b, fun(prOnto:SupervisoryAuthority(fun(prOnto:MemberState(y)))), l) AND dapreco:LetterReasonFor(l, ead))

Temporal Relations
(ALL t1)   IMPLIES 
+---   a1  
+---  (EXISTS t2)  EVENTUALLY t2 TimeRelationAfter t1
     +---   en

MFOTL Output
FORALL t1 . ALWAYS( ( EXISTS a1 . EXISTS ep . EXISTS edp . EXISTS w . EXISTS z . EXISTS x . EXISTS acm . EXISTS eac . ((prOnto:PersonalDataProcessing(ep, x, z)) AND (dapreco:AdhereTo(ead, y, acm)) AND (prOnto:nominates(edp, y, x)) AND (prOnto:PersonalData(z, w)) AND (dapreco:ApprovedCertificationMechanism(acm)))) IMPLIES ( EXISTS t2 . EXISTS en . EXISTS l . EVENTUALLY ((prOnto:Communicate(en, b, fb, l)) AND (dapreco:LetterReasonFor(l, ead)) AND (fun:prOnto:SupervisoryAuthority(fb, fa)) AND (fun:prOnto:MemberState(y, fb)))))

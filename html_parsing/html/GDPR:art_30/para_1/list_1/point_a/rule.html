Article:
Records of processing activities
Paragraph:
1. Each controller and, where applicable, the controller's representative, shall maintain a record of processing activities under its responsibility. That record shall contain all of the following information:
Point:
the name and contact details of the controller and, where applicable, the joint controller, the controller's representative and the data protection officer;

Statement 0
Raw Dapreco, Compact
EXISTS o1 . w . z . er1 . er2 . (rioOnto:RexistAtTime(o1, t1) AND prOnto:DataSubject(w) AND prOnto:PersonalData(z, w) AND prOnto:Controller(y, z) AND dapreco:Register(r) AND 'rioOnto:or(o1, er1, er2) AND 'dapreco:ResponsibleFor(er1, y, r) AND prOnto:isRepresentedBy(y, k) AND prOnto:Representative(k) AND 'dapreco:ResponsibleFor(er2, k, r) AND NAF(rioOnto:exceptionCha4Sec1Art30Par5())) IMPLIES EXISTS o2 . ec1 . ec2 . (rioOnto:RexistAtTime(o2, t1) AND 'rioOnto:or(o2, ec1, ec2) AND 'dapreco:Contain(ec1, r, fun(dapreco:contactDetails(y))) AND 'dapreco:Contain(ec2, r, fun(dapreco:contactDetails(k))))

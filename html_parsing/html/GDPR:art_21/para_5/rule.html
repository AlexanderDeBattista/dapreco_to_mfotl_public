Article:
Right to object
Paragraph:
In the context of the use of information society services, and notwithstanding Directive 2002/58/EC, the data subject may exercise his or her right to object by automated means using technical specifications.

Statement 0
Raw Dapreco, Compact
EXISTS a1 . edp . z . x . epu . (rioOnto:RexistAtTime(a1, t1) AND 'rioOnto:and(a1, ep, edp) AND prOnto:DataSubject(w) AND prOnto:PersonalData(z, w) AND prOnto:Controller(y, z) AND prOnto:Processor(x) AND 'prOnto:nominates(edp, y, x) AND 'prOnto:PersonalDataProcessing(ep, x, z) AND prOnto:Marketing(epu) AND prOnto:isBasedOn(ep, epu) AND NAF(rioOnto:exceptionCha3Sec1Art12Par2(ep))) IMPLIES EXISTS en . ew . (rioOnto:RexistAtTime(en, t1) AND 'prOnto:Communicate(en, y, w, ew) AND dapreco:electronicForm(en) AND 'dapreco:WithdrawConsent(ew, w, ep))

Temporal Relations
(ALL t1)   IMPLIES 
+---   a1  
+---   en

MFOTL Output
FORALL t1 . ALWAYS( ( EXISTS a1 . EXISTS edp . EXISTS z . EXISTS x . EXISTS epu . ((prOnto:PersonalDataProcessing(ep, x, z)) AND (prOnto:isBasedOn(ep, epu)) AND ( NOT (rioOnto:exceptionCha3Sec1Art12Par2(ep))) AND (prOnto:nominates(edp, y, x)) AND (prOnto:PersonalData(z, w)) AND (prOnto:Marketing(epu)))) IMPLIES ( EXISTS en . EXISTS ew . ((prOnto:Communicate(en, y, w, ew)) AND (dapreco:WithdrawConsent(ew, w, ep)) AND (dapreco:electronicForm(en)))))

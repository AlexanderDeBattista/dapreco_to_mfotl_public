Article:
Codes of conduct
Paragraph:
Associations and other bodies representing categories of controllers or processors may prepare codes of conduct, or amend or extend such codes, for the purpose of specifying the application of this Regulation, such as with regard to:
Point:
the collection of personal data;

(rioOnto:RexistAtTime(a1, t1) AND 'rioOnto:and(a1, ep, eor, edp) AND prOnto:DataSubject(w) AND prOnto:PersonalData(z, w) AND prOnto:Controller(y, z) AND prOnto:Processor(x) AND 'prOnto:nominates(edp, y, x) AND 'prOnto:PersonalDataProcessing(ep, x, z) AND dapreco:CategoryOf(cy, y) AND dapreco:CategoryOf(cx, x) AND 'rioOnto:or(eor, er1, er2) AND 'dapreco:Represent(er1, k, cy) AND 'dapreco:Represent(er2, k, cx)) IMPLIES EXISTS a2 . t2 . ew . ed1 . c . esi . r .
